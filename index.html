Happy Birthday ❤️

From Papa

My little princess has grown up so much!
I hope you’re doing well, and I pray everything is fine at home.
I know you must be missing me, but listen—
whenever you think of me, just look at your elder brother;
he’s just like my reflection.
If you ever need anything, just tell him,
and he’ll make sure you get it.

Never consider yourself alone,
you are papa’s strong girl after all.
Everything will be alright, just stay strong.

Enjoy your special day,
spread kindness, do some charity,
and don’t forget to keep me in your prayers.
Alhamdulillah, I am doing well. ❤️

Happy Birthday, my little star,
Look how grown and graceful you are.
Though miles apart, my love will stay,
Guiding you softly, come what may.

When you miss me, just look and see,
Your brother’s smile—it’s a part of me.
For every wish, for every dream,
He’ll bring you joy like a shining beam.

You’re papa’s strong girl, brave and true,
No storm can break the light in you.
Celebrate, smile, spread kindness today,
And in your prayers, send love my way.
